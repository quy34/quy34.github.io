Hello and welcome

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. On this page are a number of sites and projects I worked on while at Clark Community College in Vancouver Washington. these are projects that I struggled with but in the end, I became incredibly proud of.


The first piece is an SQL database I worked on. for this project, we were given the basic database and a simple frame of a website to modify and make much more useful. Specifically, we had to integrate a way to add and remove information from the database and have it displayed in real-time. As well as add the Functionality to sort and search the database in a convenient and efficient manner. The visibility and readability of the site were also left to our discretion to make it more user-friendly.


This site was made in the same class as the above and which we were to put together a full blog. site is made entirely with PHP. and I must say I'm incredibly proud of it. this project was frustrating at times because functionality was my biggest enemy. Weather in the end I managed to put together something I'm incredibly proud of and though it is not live on the internet and just uses a bunch of D&D loeum Ipsum I'm still happy with the way it looks and the way it works.


And this last site here is a very unique project for me this was made in a separate class in the same spring of 2023 in which I and a group of four others were put in charge of revitalizing the Clark College adjunct faculty portal. We were treated as a team of Freelancers hired to do a job for a site that was built the year prior and now needed some new functionality. and this project I took the role of the project manager I worked directly with our account manager and the clients themselves to relay their desires to the rest of our team so that we can make them real. I had a wonderful team and we were able to deliver our promise to revitalize this site in the ways our clients wanted
